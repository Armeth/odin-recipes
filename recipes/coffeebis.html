Tomato Flower Pie

Description

A delightfully buttery biscuit enhanced with a subtle coffee flavor.

This humble biscuit can be enjoyed with any number of hot or cold beverages, from Ishgardian tea to the richest and/or darkest Thavnairian brew.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup whole roasted coffee beans
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  1. Melt butter and add the coffee beans letting them soak in the butter for at least 1 hour on the lowest heat setting. More time soaking will yield a stronger coffee flavor.
  2. When butter is infused to your liking, strain out the beans and place in fridge to solidify.
  3. When butter is solid cream together the sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs, vanilla, flour, cornstarch and salt and mix until a crumbly dough forms.
  5. Separte the dough in balls and roll out as many cookies as desired (about 10 small cookies).
  6. Chill the dough for about 10 minutes while preheating the oven to 325 degrees.
  7. Bake the cookies for about 6 mins, rotate the pan and bake for another 6 mins or until golden brown.
